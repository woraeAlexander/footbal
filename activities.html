New Activities & Sports Programs

Introducing exciting new sports disciplines to expand our academy's offerings

In response to growing community interest and demand, SRC Sports Academy is proud to launch four new sports programs. These additions complement our existing offerings and provide even more opportunities for athletes of all levels to excel.

Our New Sports Programs

Wrestling

Training Level: Beginner to Advanced

Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday - 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Wrestling is an ancient sport that develops incredible strength, endurance, and technical skill. Our wrestling program teaches proper technique, safety protocols, and competitive strategy under the guidance of certified instructors.

  • Build explosive strength and power
  • Master fundamental wrestling techniques
  • Compete in regional and national tournaments
  • Develop mental toughness and discipline
Book Wrestling Session

Taekwondo

Training Level: Beginner to Advanced

Schedule: Tuesday, Thursday - 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art emphasizing kicks and hand techniques. Our program focuses on both self-defense and competitive sparring, with opportunities to earn belts and compete at all levels.

  • Learn authentic Korean martial arts techniques
  • Progress through belt rankings (white to black)
  • Develop flexibility, balance, and coordination
  • Build confidence and self-discipline
Book Taekwondo Session

Judo

Training Level: Beginner to Advanced

Schedule: Saturday - 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Judo, meaning "the way of gentleness," teaches throwing, grappling, and pinning techniques. It's both an effective self-defense system and an Olympic sport that emphasizes respect and mutual improvement.

  • Master throwing and grappling techniques
  • Improve balance, coordination, and body awareness
  • Participate in competitions and tournaments
  • Learn the philosophy of mutual benefit and wellbeing
Book Judo Session

Volleyball

Training Level: Beginner to Competitive

Schedule: Sunday - 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM (Competitive), Wednesday - 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM (Recreation)

Volleyball is a dynamic team sport that develops communication, coordination, and strategic thinking. Our program offers recreational leagues and competitive teams for serious athletes.

  • Master serving, passing, setting, and spiking
  • Develop team coordination and communication
  • Join recreational or competitive leagues
  • Participate in local and regional tournaments

Community Q&A Forum

Have questions about our new sports programs? Our coaches and community members are here to help!

What age groups can participate in the new sports programs?

Our programs are designed for participants aged 8 and above. Younger participants can join parent-child beginner sessions. We have training tracks for youth (8-18), adults (18-45), and seniors (45+).

Do I need any prior experience to join?

No prior experience is necessary! All four sports programs have beginner-level training. Our coaches are trained to work with complete beginners and will teach proper techniques from the ground up.

What equipment do I need to bring?

For Wrestling and Judo: Comfortable athletic wear and a mat bag. We provide mats and most equipment. For Taekwondo: Dobok (uniform) can be purchased through the academy. For Volleyball: Comfortable athletic wear and indoor sneakers. We provide balls and nets.

Are there competitive opportunities?

Yes! All four programs have competitive pathways. Participants can progress from recreational training to inter-school competitions and regional tournaments.

What are the membership/session costs?

Pricing varies by sport and level. Contact us for current rates or visit our booking page for more information. We offer monthly memberships, class packages, and pay-as-you-go options.

Can I try a free introductory session?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary introductory sessions for each new sport. Sign up to experience the training before committing to a membership.
