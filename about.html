About SRC Sports Academy

Developing young talents and promoting sports excellence within our community

The SRC Sports Academy is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing athletic talent and promoting sports development within the university and surrounding community. Founded on principles of excellence, inclusivity, and community engagement, we provide world-class training in 12 different sports disciplines.

Our Mission

To promote sports excellence and develop young talents through professional coaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community environment. We aim to inspire individuals to achieve their full potential in sports while building character, discipline, and teamwork.

Our Vision

To become a leading regional sports institution recognized for producing champions, fostering community engagement, and contributing to the development of sports at national and international levels. We envision a future where SRC Sports Academy is synonymous with athletic excellence and social responsibility.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all aspects of training and competition
  • Integrity: Acting with honesty, transparency, and fairness in all interactions
  • Community: Building strong relationships and fostering inclusive sports development
  • Respect: Honoring diversity, dignity, and the spirit of sportsmanship
  • Innovation: Continuously improving training methods and embracing modern sports science

Funding and Support

SRC Sports Academy is supported through strategic partnerships with:

  • University Student Representative Council (SRC) funding and initiatives
  • Community sports organizations and local sponsors
  • National sports bodies and athletic associations
  • Individual donors and corporate partnerships

This diverse funding model ensures financial sustainability and enables us to continuously improve our facilities and coaching programs while keeping fees affordable for all participants.

Our Team

Our coaching team consists of experienced professionals with international credentials, proven track records in competitive sports, and a passion for developing young talent. Each coach brings specialized expertise in their respective sports and a commitment to holistic athlete development.
