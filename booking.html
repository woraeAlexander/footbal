Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours before your session are fully refundable. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a 50% fee.

Can I reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 7 days before your session at no additional cost. Contact us to make changes.

Do I need prior experience?

No! We offer programs for all levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. Select your appropriate level when booking.

What should I bring?

Bring comfortable athletic wear, water bottle, and any personal equipment (if applicable). We provide most training equipment.
